== Installation ==
Using The WordPress Dashboard:
---------------------

* Go to **WordPress Admin > Plugins > Add New Plugin** from the left-hand menu.
* In the search box, type **Direct Payments for WooCommerce**.
* Click on **Install Now** when you see the Direct Payments for WooCommerce plugin.
* After installation, click **Activate**.


Manual Installation:
---------------------

* Go to **WordPress Admin > Plugins > Add New Plugin** from the left-hand menu.
* Click the **Upload Plugin** button.
* Choose `direct-payments-for-woocommerce.zip` from your computer and click **Install Now**.
* After installation, click **Activate**.


Direct Payments Setup and Configuration:
---------------------

* Go to **WooCommerce > Settings** and click on the **Payments** tab.
* Youâ€™ll see **Direct Payments for WooCommerce** listed. Click **Set Up**.
* Check the checkbox to enable Direct Payments and configure the options to your preference.
* Click **Save Changes** to update the settings and start accepting payments.
